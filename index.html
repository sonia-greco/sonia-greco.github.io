Redwood Cookies

Description

This is just a recipe to make Redwood chocolate chip cookies! :)

Inspired by

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Light brown sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Red food coloring

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and soften butter.
  2. Mix butter, all sugars, vanilla, and egg together.
  3. Add flower, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Drop in as much red food coloring as you want! (Not too much though).
  5. Roll the cookie dough into 10 or more circles, bake for around 10 minutes, and enjoy!
